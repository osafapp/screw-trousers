# Screw Trousers by Rickard Lindqvist/atacac

<img src="resources/screw-trousers.png" align="right" alt="3D Rendered Image of Screw Trousers" width="25%">

These trousers were originally designed as a part of a collection circa 2004, while Rickard was in design school. The summer before that, Rickard had been a student intern at the Vivienne Westwood studio.

> "[...] the anarchistic approach to pattern cutting there and the push and pull aesthetics in the Westwood garments had a strong influence on me. The idea was that the garments should have a shaky silhouette, as in the hand drawings accompanying Woody Guthrie's book, Bound for Glory."
> 
> *Rickard Lindqvist*

Screw Trousers was the most successful piece in that collection, which won multiple design awards. 

Rickard has been producing these trousers in several versions over the years, and still wears them himself every now and then. (He was wearing a pair at the moment he wrote the email from which I sourced the quote above.)
